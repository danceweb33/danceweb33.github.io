Classes

We offer classes for many different genres of dance including ballet, tap, jazz, hiphop, and pilates/yoga. We have multiple classes a day that range in time. Most of our classes are and hour and a half long. You would be getting taught by experienced and qualifying teachers that specialize in their genre of dance.
