Classes

We offer classes for many different genres of dance including ballet, tap, jazz, hiphop, and pilates/yoga. We have multiple classes a day that range in time. Most of our classes are and hour and a half long. You would be getting taught by experienced and qualifying teachers that specialize in their genre of dance. We also have many studio rooms of different sizes for different class sizes. You can also book a room for a Birthday Party Celebration! Just give us a call!

Environment

Join our healthy and friendly environment of supportful educators and friendly staff. Build connections, new friends, deepen bonds, and of course have fun! We are located in a new building, with modern technology and clean grounds. We have created a welcoming and supportive environment for anyone from ages 3 to 80. This facility is perfect for those who want to move around and have fun! Don't hesitate, take a class with us!

Performances

Center Line Dance Company puts together a performance once a year in the spring to show the improvement of all our dancers willing to participate in our anual recital. This performance is optional, but highly recommended for each student to show their sparkling improvement to their loved ones. The recitals are always on a Sunday with two shows: matinee and evening. We also ask for volunteers that are willing to help with costumes, food, changing room business, etc.

Common Questions Asked

The following are common questions that we get from those interested in our company:

Do you go to conventions and participate in competitions?
No, we do not participate in competition conventions. This studio is less competative and more for the fun of dance and movement. It's a great place to form bonds and friendships, not compete against your peers and create an evironment with tension.
Are we supposed to buy our costumes for the shows, or are the costumes provided by the studio?
It depends on the dances and age group, but we tend to reuse costumes because it's more cost friendly and better for the environment. However, there are some occasions where new costumes are needed to be bought by the dancer using them.
How are classes arranged? By age? By skill?
There are 3 different categories: A(adults), B(babies), and C(children). Each category has multiple levels that people are placed in considering age, experience, and skill. People must participate in a placement test in order for the teachers to evaluate and place each person in a level.
